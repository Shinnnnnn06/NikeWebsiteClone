WHAT IS NIKE MEMBERSHIP?

  • Free shipping (and returns). Just sign in when you shop and you’ll automatically get free standard shipping and free shipping for returns.
  • Member Shop. A unique collection of Nike shoes and gear that is only available to Nike Members.
  • Deals. Special offers and promos to thank our Members and to celebrate big moments, like birthdays.
